Instructions


1. Enter in your email

2. Use the Search bar to find specific areas

3. Select either a point or rectangle on the map and enter in the:

  • location type(work,school, etc)
  • the location name
  • and how often you visit the location in a week

4. Use the delete button on the toolbar if an error is made

5. Click on the blue submit bar at the bottom of the page to submit your survey
