About The Project

What is this?


This web map is intended for investors in alcoholic production and distribution, Researchers interested in alcoholic consumption and demand, and general alcohol enthusiasts! This app allows you to select between Irish Whiskey, Wine, Beer, and Spirits. Within the Irish Whiskey category, you can also select standard, premium, or ultra-premium. The time slider on the bottom will allow you to select a year going from 1835 to 2019, and then the chloropleth map will change to reflect the results of that query. More specific information will be displayed on the upper left, while the histogram and frequency distribution charts will also change to accomodate your selection.

Datasets:

The ISWR drinks analysis organization provided data on Irish Whiskey Sales worldwide, their data can be found on data.world

All other data was provided by the University of Adelaide who host a wine megafile of alcohol consumption, production, exportation, and importation. The data used in this project was the national consumption per year as that is a fairly close estimation of demand in that country. The University of Adelaide's wine megafile can be found here
