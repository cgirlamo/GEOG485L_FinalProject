Chris Girlamo

I am a prospective graduate student at the University of New Mexico. I am currently finishing a dual degree program where I will recieve a Bachelors of Business Administration in Management Information Systems and a Bachelors of Science in Geography. I am currently employed by Dr. Yan Lin of UNM as a research assisstant. I have assisted her in analyzing spatial patterns using network analysis and gathering and cleaning data related to wind patterns. I also work as an intern at the Earth Data Analysis Center where I assist GIS analysts in analyzing and visually representing spatial data as well as cleaning and providing data for customers.
